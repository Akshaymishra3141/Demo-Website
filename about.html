About Us

We're Building the Future of Agent-Driven Development

Founded by engineers and AI researchers, Notus was born out of a simple frustration: building intelligent systems still required too much manual orchestration.

We set out to change that by creating a tool that lets teams design, simulate, and launch autonomous agents visually and intuitively.

Today, Notus powers next-gen workflows for startups, dev teams, and AI-first platforms across the globe. Whether you're automating internal ops or scaling customer support, we provide the infrastructure for what's next.
